[二叉树的中序遍历](https://leetcode-cn.com/problems/binary-tree-inorder-traversal/)

<p>给定一个二叉树，返回它的<em>中序&nbsp;</em>遍历。</p>

<!-- more -->

<p><strong>示例:</strong></p>

<pre><strong>输入:</strong> [1,null,2,3]
   1
    \
     2
    /
   3

<strong>输出:</strong> [1,3,2]</pre>
