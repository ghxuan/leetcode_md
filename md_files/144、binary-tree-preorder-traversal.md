[二叉树的前序遍历](https://leetcode-cn.com/problems/binary-tree-preorder-traversal/)

<!-- more -->

<p>&nbsp;<strong>示例:</strong></p>

<pre><strong>输入:</strong> [1,null,2,3]

    \
     2
    /
   3 

<strong>输出:</strong> [1,2,3]
</pre>
